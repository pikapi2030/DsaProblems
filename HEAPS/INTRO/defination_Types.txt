. a heap is a complete binary tree(CBT)
. a CBT is a BT with all leves fiiled completly except the last level(can be filled),
insertion of nodes should be from left side first

Types Of Heaps
Min Heap:-
parent node is smaller then both child nodes(applicable for every  node)

Max Heap :-
parentnode is larger than both child nodes(applicable for every node)
------------------------------------------
Insertion:-
Brute way:
to insert a node,we first have to find an empty space in heap to insert(from left)(to fulfil CBT)
then after insertion, we have to satisfy conditon of min or max heap
for ex:- in a max heap, if we insert 59
it would be at bottom before restoring the property of heaps,
we will swap the parent node of 59 with 59  till  parent is smaller then 59 
T.C:-  O(n)(to find the empty space for insertion) +O(height)(swaping)
S.C:- O(1)
better way:-
we use an array to reduce T.C of insertion

array contains elements to be inserted in order( indexed from 0)

parent of ith node= (i-1)/2 (floor) 
child of ith node= 2*i+1(left), (2*i)+2 (right)
